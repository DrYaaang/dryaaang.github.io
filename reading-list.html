Reading List

Sapiens

Yuval Noah Harari

A broad and thought-provoking history of humankind. I appreciated how it connects biology, culture, and economics in one narrative.

Thinking, Fast and Slow

Daniel Kahneman

Dense but rewarding. It helped me better recognize cognitive bias in daily decisions and technical judgment.

Atomic Habits

James Clear

Practical and clear. The habit loop and environment design ideas were the most useful for building consistent daily routines.

The Culture Map

Erin Meyer

Very relevant for international collaboration. The communication framework is practical and easy to apply in real teamwork.
